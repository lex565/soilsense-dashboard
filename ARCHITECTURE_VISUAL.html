Why This Design?

❌ Why NOT just SMAP + SMS?

  • SMAP 9-km too coarse (covers entire farm)
  • Farmers can't see which plots need water
  • Result: wasteful blanket irrigation

✓ Why 10-m + 4 Layers?

  • 10-m aligns with plot size (0.5-2 ha)
  • Layer 3 validation builds farmer trust
  • Layer 4 SMS reaches feature phones (no internet)
